OUR HISTORY & APPROACH
Simple is a nationally-recognised integrated marketing agency based in Adelaide, South Australia. If you're looking for a forward-thinking, results-driven agency to deliver your next project, then you've come to the right place.

EXPERIENCED DELIVERING HIGH PROFILE AND COMPLEX PROJECTS
Simple is highly experienced delivering large scale and complex projects to high profile clients nationally. Our clients span a broad range of industry sectors, including food, health, government, property, construction, professional services and education. We are commercially focused and have a proven track record of delivering value to and increasing the profitability of our clients.

A DECADE OF AWARDS
As an agency, our work has been recognised at industry awards every year since our inception in 2005, highlighting the high standard of work we consistently deliver to our clients. This hasn’t been just for small, highly creative pieces, but includes large information-driven websites such as the City of Marion’s website in 2012 and City of Port Augusta’s website in 2013.

OUR TEAM
Great people make great agencies. Our team is comprised of highly experienced specialists spanning a full range of marketing and design disciplines, such as brand identity, graphic design, website design, packaging and advertising. Our team consists of project managers, strategists, designers, developers, copywriters and illustrators who collaborate to deliver high quality project outcomes.

LOCAL AND APPROACHABLE
Based centrally on King William Road at Unley, we’re truly a local agency. We have an open door policy and a dedicated team of senior designers, developers, project managers and account managers to support the projects we deliver and to build long term relationships with our clients. As an agency, we focus on relationships, not on short term projects.

NATIONAL REACH
We’re based in Adelaide, but work with clients in every corner of Australia – and beyond. We’re supported by technology, a national network of production partners and a willingness to travel when it’s needed to deliver a project. Our extensive list of national clients, spanning regional councils through to national multi site retailers, demonstrates our ability to work as effectively with clients thousands of kilometres away as we do with those that are literally around the corner.
