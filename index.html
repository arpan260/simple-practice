GRAPHIC DESIGN & WEB DESIGN ADELAIDE
Simple is a national integrated marketing agency based in Adelaide. Our team is comprised of specialists spanning a full range of marketing disciplines. We’re effectively a graphic design, digital (web design) and advertising agency all rolled into one. We work with clients spanning all sizes, industry sectors and geographic locations. Our clients include federal, state and local governments, education providers and major organisations through to local, family-owned businesses and start-ups.

AWARD WINNING RESULTS
Our work has been recognised at industry awards every year since our inception, highlighting the high standard of work we consistently deliver. Our work has been recognised across a broad range of design categories, including brand identity, graphic design, packaging, publication, website design and development and more. We’re grounded by strategy, but are quick to break convention. We’re leaders, not followers. We are commercially focused and have a proven track record of delivering value to and increasing the profitability of our clients.

INTEGRATED SERVICE OFFERING
Whether the requirement is a brand identity, user-friendly website or printed communication, we bring together commercial insight, strategy, ideas and beautifully executed design work to help our clients effectively reach and engage with their audiences.

OUR APPROACH
We can be engaged to deliver an individual graphic design project, such as a brand identity, website or packaging system, or to deliver an integrated project spanning multiple marketing disciplines in parallel. Regardless of the basis on which we work, we focus on building long term relationships rather than on short term projects. This is reflected by the close working relationships we maintain with our clients - many of which we have now worked with for a decade.
